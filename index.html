About Us

Bright Industrial Consultancy Service is a dynamic engineering consultancy specializing in Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and industrial inspection services. Our team of certified experts delivers comprehensive asset integrity solutions that prioritize safety, quality and efficiency.

We serve industries like Oil & Gas, Power Generation, Petrochemicals, Fertilizers, and more. Our mission is to help clients detect problems early, extend equipment life, and stay fully compliant with international safety and quality standards.
